Simple Mac and Cheese

Simple Mac and Cheese is an alternative to boxed mac and cheese that's so simple yet so good. It only takes around 25 minutes to make and is perfect for those busy weeknights. It's so simple and easy that you'll probably have it memorized after just a few dinners.

Ingredients

  • 8oz box of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni until al dente, this takes about 8 minutes. For thicker mac and cheese: Double the amount of macaroni.
  2. While the macaroni is cooking, melt butter in a saucepan on medium heat
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper, then stir until smooth. This takes around 5 minutes.
  4. Slowly add milk while stiring continuously. Cook until mixture is smooth and bubbling while making sure the milk doesn't burn. This also takes around 5 minutes.
  5. Add cheddar cheese until melted, 2 to 4 minutes.
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce unti it's coated.
  7. Serve while it's hot and enjoy!
