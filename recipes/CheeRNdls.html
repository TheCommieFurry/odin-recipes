Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

Cheesy Ramen Noodles is a cheap and easy way to make your ramen noodle dinner just oh so much better. Giving it a thick and creamy texture and sauce. It's cheap and quick too!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 pack of any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice of American cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot until boiling
  2. Add ramen noodles to pot and cook until soft, around 3 minutes
  3. Add seasoning packet to the pot
  4. Add cheese to the pot and mix until it has melted
  5. Plate and enjoy!
